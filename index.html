About Bharat POPAT

Bharat Popat is a pioneering entrepreneur and investor with a track record of building and scaling disruptive companies across multiple industries. With a keen eye for emerging technologies and market opportunities, Bharat has established himself as a forward-thinking business leader.

Starting his entrepreneurial journey at the age of 22, Bharat has founded companies in technology, renewable energy, and financial services, creating substantial value and employment opportunities worldwide.

His philosophy centers on sustainable growth, technological innovation, and creating solutions that address humanity's most pressing challenges.
