About Mr. Bharat Popat

Mr. Bharat Popat is the founder promoter of Ameya Information Ltd. A dynamic personality and a great visionary, he has a rich experience of over two decades. He started his career as a trader in 1987.

Soon after, he went on to establish the Benefit Group. During the course of his career, he has been associated with various national and international business houses.

With an in-depth understanding of the market pulse, he has released several print directories catering to certain geographies and categories in the last two decades.

He has instituted Indian Business Pages with a mission to bridge the gap between the buyer and supplier on a pan India basis.
